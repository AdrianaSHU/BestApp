About Our Company

Best Confectionary is a well-established confectionery manufacturer with roots dating back to 1920. Situated in Sheffield, the company has earned a reputation for excellence in the industry. Specializing in the production of small packs of hard-boiled sweets, it caters primarily to major retailers. With a dedicated workforce of 20-25 employees, Best Confectionary operates across multiple functions, including administration, packing, goods handling, and production.

About Our App

With a focus on real-time visibility, it aims to enhance safety monitoring and prevent future accidents. The app seeks to view humidity levels in the drying room and in the main area. It focuses on waste and stock control, inventory management, and improving product movement and security. For Security, the app targets unauthorized access risks with security devices and aims to streamline operations with user-friendly applications for all employees.
